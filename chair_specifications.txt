PREMIUM WOODEN CHAIR - VISUAL SPECIFICATIONS
==============================================

PROJECT: Landing Page Hero Visual
PURPOSE: First attraction for furniture marketplace website
STYLE: Ultra-premium, modern, dynamic product photography

==============================================
TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
==============================================

RESOLUTION:
- Output: 3840 x 2160 pixels (4K UHD)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- DPI: 72 (web) / 300 (print if needed)
- Format: PNG with alpha transparency

RENDER SETTINGS:
- Engine: Cycles
- Samples: 256 (minimum) / 512 (optimal)
- Denoising: Enabled
- Color Space: sRGB
- View Transform: Filmic
- Look: High Contrast

==============================================
CHAIR DESIGN REQUIREMENTS
==============================================

STYLE:
- Modern contemporary design
- Clean lines and refined edges
- Premium craftsmanship visible
- Suitable for high-end furniture marketplace

WOOD TYPE:
- Rich brown wooden finish
- Natural wood grain visible
- Polished/satin finish (not matte, not glossy)
- Premium hardwood appearance (walnut, oak, or mahogany style)

COMPONENTS (Must be separate objects):
1. Four legs (individual objects)
2. Seat base
3. Backrest
4. Optional: Armrests (if design includes)
5. Optional: Support bars/stretchers

PROPORTIONS:
- Realistic human-scale dimensions
- Seat height: ~45-50cm from ground
- Overall height: ~80-95cm
- Seat depth: ~40-45cm
- Width: ~45-55cm

==============================================
VISUAL COMPOSITION
==============================================

CAMERA:
- View: 3/4 perspective (45° horizontal angle)
- Height: Slightly above seat level (20° vertical)
- Focal Length: 85mm (portrait lens)
- Distance: 5 units from origin
- Framing: Chair centered, balanced spacing

BACKGROUND:
- Pure white (#FFFFFF)
- No gradients or textures
- Clean and minimal
- Suitable for web overlay text

LIGHTING SETUP:
- Style: Professional studio HDR lighting
- Key Light: Top-right, 45° angle, warm tone (1000W equivalent)
- Fill Light: Left side, softer, cool tone (300W equivalent)
- Rim Light: Back, creates edge definition (500W equivalent)
- Shadows: Soft, realistic, subtle
- Reflections: Gentle on polished wood surfaces

==============================================
EXPLODED/ASSEMBLY VIEW
==============================================

CONCEPT:
- Semi-exploded state showing chair parts
- Parts appear to be floating and about to assemble
- Smooth, intentional spacing (not chaotic)
- Suitable for "parts coming together" animation

SPACING:
- Explosion distance: 0.3 units (adjustable)
- Parts aligned on assembly axis
- Maintain visual connection between parts
- Legs slightly separated from seat
- Backrest floating above seat
- All parts facing correct assembly direction

VISUAL INTENT:
- Dynamic and modern
- Shows craftsmanship and construction
- Implies customization and quality
- Creates visual interest and movement

==============================================
MATERIAL SPECIFICATIONS
==============================================

WOOD MATERIAL:
- Base Color: Rich brown (RGB: 64, 38, 20 approximately)
- Metallic: 0.0 (wood is non-metallic)
- Specular: 0.5 (moderate reflectivity)
- Roughness: 0.15 (polished finish)
- Clearcoat: 0.3 (extra polish layer)
- Clearcoat Roughness: 0.1

WOOD GRAIN:
- Procedural wave texture
- Scale: 15.0
- Distortion: 2.0
- Detail: 3.0
- Direction: Following wood grain natural flow
- Variation: Subtle color variation between grain lines

FINISH:
- Polished but not mirror-like
- Visible reflections of environment
- Soft highlights on edges
- Natural wood character preserved
